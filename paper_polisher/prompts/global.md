# Global Academic Writing Policy

This policy applies to all sections of the manuscript.

## 1. Native Tone & Rhythm
- Rewrite text to sound like a native researcher in Computer Vision.
- Vary sentence structure to avoid monotony.
- Prioritize directness and technical clarity.

## 2. Anti-AI Style
- Strictly suppress the use of banned patterns found in `banned_patterns.yaml`.
- Replace generic "AI-polished" phrases with technically grounded alternatives.
- Avoid over-explaining common concepts.

## 3. Compression Engine
- Aggressively reduce word count without losing technical substance.
- Eliminate "survey-style" narration in research papers.
- Aim for high information density.

## 4. Equation Preservation
- DO NOT touch mathematical symbols, equations, or references.
- Ensure the text around equations flows naturally and connects logically to the math.
