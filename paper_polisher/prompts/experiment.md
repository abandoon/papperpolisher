# Experiment Writing Policy

## Objective: Analysis Over Promotion

### 1. Insightful Interpretation
- Explain *why* the results are better, rather than just stating they are.
- Focus on the behavior of the model under different conditions.

### 2. Failure Analysis
- Don't hide weaknesses; explain them. This builds reviewer trust.
- Identify the bounds of the proposed method.

### 3. Avoid "Reading Numbers"
- Do not narrate the tables in text (e.g., "Method A got 95%, Method B got 96%").
- Instead, highlight trends and key takeaways.

### 4. Comparison Pacing
- Group methods logically in comparisons.
- Use precise verbs for comparisons (e.g., "surpasses", "aligns with", "exhibits").
