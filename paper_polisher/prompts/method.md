# Methodology Writing Policy

## Objective: Equation-grounded Engineering Clarity

### 1. Mathematical Rigor
- Text should justify and explain the equations, not just repeat them.
- Maintain consistent notation throughout the section.

### 2. Engineering Clarity
- Describe the architecture/algorithm with precision.
- Avoid philosophical interpretations or inflated abstractions.

### 3. Logic Flow
- Ensure a smooth transition between different components of the system.
- Use technical terminology correctly (e.g., specific loss functions, layers, or optimization steps).
