# NeurIPS/ICLR Style Prior

## Rhetorical Distribution
- **Sentence Length**: Dense and compact.
- **Equation Density**: High; prioritizes formal definitions.
- **Notation Compactness**: Extremely high.
- **Tone**: Technically rigorous and objective.
- **Implementation Detail Ratio**: Low in main text; focus on mathematical formulation.
- **Wording Density**: High; every word must carry weight.
