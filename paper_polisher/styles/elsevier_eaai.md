# Elsevier / EAAI Style Prior

## Rhetorical Distribution
- **Sentence Length**: Primarily concise and direct. Avoids overly complex nesting.
- **Tone**: Professional, objective, and "engineering-focused". 
- **Application Emphasis**: High density of words relating to "evaluation", "development", "implementation", and "performance".
- **Intro Pacing**: Steady. Provides a clear path from the engineering problem to the AI solution.
- **Discussion Style**: Focuses on practical implications, computational efficiency, and real-world applicability.
- **Claim Style**: Evidence-based and assertive.

## Common Phrasing Patterns
- "Evaluation of [Method] using [Dataset]..."
- "This study proposes a [Architecture] to address the challenge of..."
- "Experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of..."
- "The proposed framework achieves a balance between [Metric A] and [Metric B]."

## Domain Alignment (AD & Diffusion)
- Uses precise terms: "reconstruction fidelity", "semantic discrepancy", "noise-to-norm paradigm", "reverse denoising process".
- Emphasizes the "trade-off" between reconstruction quality and anomaly sensitivity.
