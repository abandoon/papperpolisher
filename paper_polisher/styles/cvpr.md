# CVPR/ICCV Style Prior

## Rhetorical Distribution
- **Sentence Length**: Short and punchy.
- **Tone**: Direct and assertive.
- **Intro Pacing**: Fast; gets to the contribution within the first page.
- **Transition Density**: Low; relies on logical structure rather than transition words.
- **Background Narration**: Minimal.
- **Claim Style**: Bold but supported.
