# TPAMI/IJCV Style Prior

## Rhetorical Distribution
- **Sentence Length**: Medium to long; more complex clauses.
- **Tone**: Highly formal and comprehensive.
- **Intro Pacing**: Moderate; provides thorough context and broad motivation.
- **Discussion Depth**: High; significant focus on theoretical justification and edge cases.
- **Transition Density**: Moderate.
- **Claim Style**: Restrained and nuanced.
